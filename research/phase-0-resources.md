## Learning Path Recommendation

If you're working through these topics sequentially as a "Phase 0" foundation:

1. **Start with Topic 1** — watch one intro video or read one article to understand what data analytics is.
2. **Then Topic 6** — learn basic database concepts conceptually (read the IBM articles before touching SQL).
3. **Then Topic 3** — understand dimensions, measures, and granularity through Kimball Group.
4. **Then Topic 7** — structured vs unstructured + OLTP vs OLAP (IBM articles).
5. **Then Topic 5** — the four types of analytics (Chartio article is the quickest win).
6. **Then Topic 4** — the full data lifecycle (IBM lifecycle article).
7. **Then Topic 2** — analyst thinking (SQLTeaching.com is a great practical intro).
8. **Finally Topic 8** — data quality (IBM articles), because it ties everything together.

**Estimated total time:** 15–30 hours to work through all resources at a comfortable pace.
