# NZ Data Analyst Job Market — Skills Analysis

**Date:** 22 June 2026
**Sources:** Seek.co.nz (~400-1,300 listings), Indeed.nz (~50+ with JDs), LinkedIn.nz (~190 listings)
**Focus:** Mid-to-senior level Data Analyst roles (excluding Junior/Graduate/Intern)
**Sample:** 60+ listings analyzed (50 with skills data)

## The Modern NZ Data Stack

**Consistent pattern across forward-looking roles:**
```
SQL → Snowflake → dbt → Power BI
```

This stack appeared in: Health NZ, DOC, Waste Management, Barker's, ASB, Stats NZ, Mercury, Meridian, EY NZ, Absolute IT.

| Requirement | Context |
|---|---|
| **Te Tiriti o Waitangi / Te Ao Māori** | Required in all government roles. Stats NZ uses te reo Māori job titles. Department of Conservation lists Māori data sovereignty. |
| **NZ Government security clearance** | Common in Wellington gov roles (GCSB, Corrections, MSD). |
| **Chinese/English bilingual** | Rare but present (Atom Intelligence, Auckland). |

## Employer Distribution

- **Government:** ~40% of listings (Stats NZ, Health NZ, MSD, DOC, Corrections, ERO, NZQA, Kainga Ora, Customs, NH Commission)
- **Private:** ~60% (Banks, telcos, consultancies, tech companies)
- **Location:** Wellington (~40%), Auckland (~40%), Christchurch (~15%), Other (~5%)
- **Working model:** Hybrid is standard (~70%). Fully remote is rare (~2-3 listings).

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **SQL is non-negotiable.** If you invest in only one technical skill, make it SQL.
2. **Power BI dominates Tableau in NZ.**
3. **The modern stack is Snowflake + dbt.** Learn this combo.
4. **Python is becoming baseline for mid-level roles.**
5. **Government is the biggest DA employer.** Emphasize conceptual thinking, stakeholder work, Te Tiriti.
6. **"Full data lifecycle" is expected.** Analysts go from raw data → stakeholder insights.
7. **Contract/temp is common** (6-month to 2-year fixed terms, especially government).

## Typical Job Description (Stats NZ)

> **Data Analyst / Kaitatari Hoahoa**
> **Salary:** $84,027–$98,855 | **Type:** 2-year fixed term
>
> *Help modernise core economic statistics. Identify opportunities to streamline processes, integrate data across systems, and make effective use of automation and AI.*
>
> **You will:**
> - Contribute to production of high-quality statistical products
> - Analyse data across core releases, identifying trends
> - Ensure outputs are accurate and robust
> - Work across multiple data sources and systems
> - Identify opportunities to automate processes, including AI
>
> **Requirements:**
> - Qualifications in economics, finance, statistics, or related
> - Strong analytical and data skills
> - Experience across multiple datasets/systems
> - Sound judgement, comfort with ambiguity
> - Collaborative mindset
> - 4-6 years experience
