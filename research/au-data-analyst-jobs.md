## Key AU Trends

1. **"Modern Data Stack" is firmly in AU:** Snowflake + dbt + Power BI becoming default.
2. **Power BI hegemony:** ~65% vs Tableau ~15%. Microsoft ecosystem lock-in (Azure + PBI + Excel + Fabric).
3. **Python crossing the chasm:** Moving from "nice to have" to "required" in 35% of mid/senior DA roles.
4. **AI/ML aspirational:** Appearing in JDs as "AI initiatives" but few ask for deep ML engineering.
5. **Data quality/governance emerging as specialization:** Dedicated roles with Ataccama, specific DQ tools.
6. **Contract market is hot:** Many 6-12 month contracts at premium rates.
7. **"Data + domain" specialization:** Healthcare, supply chain, financial services, education.
